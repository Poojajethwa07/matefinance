History of MATE

  • 2024
    Founding of MATE

    Founded by brothers Manish Patel (B.E. Computer Science) and Ravish Patel (B.E. Electronics), MATE (MultiAsset Traders Education) emerged to tackle the high failure rate in trading.

  • Early Growth
    Zero Trading Loss Philosophy

    Their shared vision of achieving zero trading loss became MATE’s foundation. They leveraged finance, technology, and education to build a unique curriculum and strategy.

  • Expansion
    Gaining Recognition

    Starting with a small community, MATE quickly earned a reputation for practical and result-driven trading methodologies.

  • Present
    Leading Fintech Education

    Today, MATE provides expert training and innovative fintech solutions, fostering trader excellence and financial literacy.

Our Vision and Mission

Vision

Our vision is to become the leading global provider of transformative trading education, renowned for its high success rates and positive impact on the lives of our students. We envision a future where financial literacy is widespread, and our students are not only financially successful but also active, engaged members of the trading community. We believe that by equipping individuals with the knowledge and skills to succeed in trading, we contribute to a more informed and empowered global financial landscape. We strive to create a diverse, inclusive, and supportive community where individuals from all backgrounds have the opportunity to achieve their financial goals.

Mission

Our mission at MATE (MultiAsset Traders Education) is to empower individuals to achieve financial success through comprehensive, practical, and results-oriented trading education. We strive to achieve this by providing accessible and innovative educational resources, fostering a supportive learning community, and consistently adapting our curriculum to meet the ever-evolving needs of the financial markets. Our commitment extends beyond mere theoretical knowledge; we prioritize practical application and hands-on experience, equipping traders with the tools and confidence they need to navigate the complexities of trading with a keen focus on risk management and loss prevention. We believe in ethical and responsible trading practices and promote transparency in all our operations.
